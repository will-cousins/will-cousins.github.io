This is the home page of Will Cousins. I was formerly a Postdoctoral Associate in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. I worked with Themis Sapsis on studying the dynamics and statistics of extreme ocean waves. Our work has been reported on by The Economist and Popular Mechanics. You can find out more about our work by visiting the SAND lab home page.

Prior to working at MIT, I obtained my Ph.D. in Applied Mathematics from North Carolina State University, where I developed tools for modeling blood flow in trees of millions of arteries
